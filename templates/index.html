Welcome to the Sales Data Insights Provider!

This application helps companies gain valuable insights from their sales data. When you submit your data, we provide you with detailed plots and analyses to enhance your business strategies.

What We Offer

  1. Sales Analysis by Hour: Identify peak sales hours for effective scheduling of advertisements and promotions.
  2. Monthly Sales Analysis: Evaluate sales trends over each month for strategic planning of promotions and advertising campaigns.
  3. City-wise Sales Analysis: Gain insights into sales trends across different cities for targeted marketing and promotions.
  4. Category-wise Sales Analysis: Understand which product categories are the top sellers for strategic marketing and promotions.
  5. Most Profitable Products: Discover which products contribute the most to your profits.
  6. Discover the most frequently paired products that customers buy together
  7. Analysis of Sales by Quantity Ordered: Determine the busiest periods for optimizing advertising and promotional activities.

How to Use

  1. Please ensure that the dataset includes the following columns, and maintain the column names as follows: Order ID, Product, Quantity Ordered, Price Each, Order Date, Purchase Address, and Category.
  2. Upload CSV file and click on SUBMIT
  3. Kindly hold on for a moment as we're processing a large dataset.
  4. Receive Customized Analysis: Once you submit your data, our application will generate interactive plots and analyses tailored to your sales information.
  5. Gain Valuable Insights: Explore the provided visualizations to uncover valuable insights about your sales performance.
